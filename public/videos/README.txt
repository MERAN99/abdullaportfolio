VIDEO BACKGROUND INSTRUCTIONS

To use your space video as a background:

1. Place your space background video in this folder (public/videos/)
2. Rename your video to "space-background.mp4"
3. Make sure it's in MP4 format
4. Refresh the page in your browser

If you don't have a space video, you can download one from:
- Pexels: https://www.pexels.com/search/videos/space/
- Pixabay: https://pixabay.com/videos/search/space/
- Videvo: https://www.videvo.net/stock-video-footage/space/

After downloading, rename it to "space-background.mp4" and place it in this folder.
